Research Interests

Additive Manufacturing Security
The additive manufacturing industry is booming, but the proliferation of unchecked digital model files can become a serious security problem.
My research is focused on securing the contents of these digital files, and ensuring the additive manufacturing process is secure.
The research problems I study cover areas of computer science, mathematics, and engineering, including software engineering, algorithm design and analysis, and computational geometry.
